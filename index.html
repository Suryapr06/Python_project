SIL Mauritius Legacy

Centred on Governance, Driven by Agility

SILDIA Informatics India Private Limited was established in Hyderabad as a Global Capability Center (GCC) for State Informatics Limited (SIL), Mauritius. SIL is a highly-esteemed provider of enterprise IT and digital government architectures in Mauritius and the African continent.

By combining the institutional governance and regional expertise of SIL Mauritius with the dynamic IT talent pool and modern engineering agility of Hyderabad, SILDIA builds robust, secure solutions that power next-generation operations.

  • Make In India Advantage: Harnessing elite local developers, system engineers, and AI architects to deliver globally-scaled systems.
  • Sub-Saharan African Presence: Serving as the technical delivery engine for foundational government projects and banking architectures in Africa.
  • Trust & Integrity: Aligning with international compliance models, strict cybersecurity architectures, and rigorous testing schedules.
